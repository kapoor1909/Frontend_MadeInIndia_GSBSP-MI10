ABOUT US

This website supports the “Made in India” initiative by the Government of India scheme launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in 2014 intended to boost the domestic manufacturing sector and also augment investment into the country. It comprehensively covers the details and benefits of the products that are manufactured by industries and local aritsans that hail from India. It is also meant for improving the market of basic products in the country to create a global hub for business.
